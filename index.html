Your Math Professor on the Web!

I am a highly experienced Math Professor with two doctoral degrees: A Ph.D. in Mathematics (IIT) and a Ph.D. in Computer Science (Rutgers). Time flies--it's been 7 years since I started a hiatus from my academic career and started homeschooling my mathematically gifted youngster. Last year, in her 7th grade, she aced the AP Calc BC course with a perfect 5/5. She's now chugging along studying Linear Algebra and Multivariable Calculus.

I can teach students of any level of academic readiness and at any level of education, from elementary school to college. My philosophy for teaching Math is simple: Everything in Math makes logical sense. If you understand why something makes sense, you will never need to learn by rote. I also strongly believe that practice in Math is as important as practice in sports and music. Patience is my forte and my methods remove students' fear of Math and see why learning can be fun.

Email me your Math questions, I will post my answers on these pages (time permitting).
