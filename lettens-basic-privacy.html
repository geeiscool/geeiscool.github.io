Lettens App Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which we handle user data for the mobile application "Lettens Basic" (the "App").

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from its users. We do not require you to provide any personal information, and we do not track your location, your device's unique identifier, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. No Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or other services that could collect your data. This includes, but is not limited to, services like Google Analytics, Firebase, or other tracking frameworks.

3. Data Storage

All data created or used by the App is stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to this data, nor is it ever sent to our servers or any third-party servers.

4. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no security risks associated with the transmission of your data from the App to us or any third parties. The security of your data on your device is protected by the built-in security features of the iOS operating system.

5. Crash Logs and Analytics

We may receive anonymized crash logs and app analytics data from Apple. This data is not personally identifiable and is used solely for the purpose of improving the stability and performance of the App. This data is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to periodically review this Privacy Policy for any changes.
